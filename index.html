    🤵🏻 About Me

    I am a machine learning researcher specializing in spatio-temporal data mining and time series forecasting. I obtained my doctoral degree from the Big Data College at the University of Science and Technology of China in June 2024. I had the privilege of being supervised by Professor Lian Defu and Professor Wang Yang. Currently, I am an assistant professor at the Suzhou Institute for Advanced Study at the University of Science and Technology of China.
